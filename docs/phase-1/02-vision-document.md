# Vision Document

## Vision

SkillForge Enterprise helps organizations run secure, reusable, branded internal assessments without the cost and rigidity of recruitment-first assessment platforms.

## Problem

Many assessment platforms are optimized for hiring screens. Internal learning teams need recurring certification, training validation, batch-based reporting, trainer-owned question workflows, reusable templates, and flexible branding. They also need lower operational overhead for invitations, reminders, reports, and candidate support.

## Product Promise

SkillForge Enterprise gives enterprises a governed question bank, flexible assessment builder, secure candidate experience, live trainer dashboard, rich analytics, and integration-ready APIs in one deployable platform.

## Target Users

- Corporate learning and development teams.
- Technical trainers.
- HR and talent development teams.
- Universities and bootcamps.
- Training institutes.
- Enterprise administrators.
- Candidates and learners.

## Success Metrics

- 80 percent reduction in manual invitation and score-reporting effort.
- 90 percent of recurring assessments created from templates.
- Reports generated in under 30 seconds for standard cohorts.
- Candidate completion rate above 95 percent for stable network conditions.
- Zero cross-tenant data exposure in security tests.
